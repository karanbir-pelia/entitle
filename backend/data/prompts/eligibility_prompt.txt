Each program includes monthly_benefit_range. Use that range to estimate estimated_monthly_value_usd:
- If typical_usd is present, use that as the estimate.
- If typical_family_4_usd is present and the household is close to 4 people, use that as a guide and adjust conservatively for household size.
- If only min_usd and max_usd are present, choose a conservative midpoint or lower-middle estimate based on income and household size.

- For one-time or seasonal benefits, convert to a monthly equivalent.
- Do not leave estimated_monthly_value_usd null unless no dollar estimate is possible.
