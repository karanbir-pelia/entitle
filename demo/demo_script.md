# Entitle Demo Script

Target length: 90 seconds.

## Scene 1: Privacy

Open Entitle and show the local privacy badge.

Narration: "Entitle runs locally with Gemma 4 through Ollama, so sensitive benefit information stays on the user's device."

## Scene 2: Benefits Discovery

Type:

```text
Hi, I'm a single mom with 2 kids. I work part time at Target and bring home about $1,400 a month. We live in San Antonio, Texas. We do not have health insurance and energy bills have been hard.
```

If Entitle asks for ages, type:

```text
My kids are 2 and 6.
```

Show Entitle moving from intake to eligibility results.

## Scene 3: Results

Open the Results tab. Show state-specific programs and the estimated monthly total.

Narration: "The app turns a plain-language situation into a prioritized checklist: food help, health coverage, WIC, energy assistance, phone or internet discounts, and application steps."

## Scene 4: Application Guide

Expand one card and open the application guide. Show documents and next steps.

Narration: "Entitle does not submit forms. It prepares the user to apply with the right documents and local application path."

## Scene 5: Document Reader

Open the Document tab and upload a sample renewal notice or denial letter.

Narration: "The vision flow explains official notices in plain language, including deadlines and appeal options."

## Scene 6: Multilingual

Switch language to Spanish and type:

```text
Hola, somos 3 personas, gano $1500 al mes y vivimos en Texas.
```

Close on the Spanish response or results screen.
